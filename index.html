history

Equipping all items in the set increases the Engineer's vocalizations pitch by 145%.

This set includes Halloween-restricted items and can only be worn around Halloween or during a Full Moon; at other times of the year, it does not appear in gameplay or grant its effect, unless the server has Halloween mode enabled. However, it can be equipped at any time and can still be viewed on the loadout screen even when it is not visible during games.

Update history

October 27, 2011 Patch (Very Scary Halloween Special) The Brundle Bundle was added to the game.

Bugs

Spies disguised as an Engineer using the Brundle Bundle set do not have high-pitched voice commands.

With this set equipped, the Engineer's death sounds do not change pitch if he is killed.

The sounds of special taunts such as the Rancho Relaxo and the Dueling Banjo are affected by the pitch change of this set, even though they are not spoken Engineer lines.
