history

Equipping all items in the set allows the Heavy to perform the FrankenHeavy taunt.

This set includes Halloween-restricted items and can only be worn around Halloween or during a Full Moon; at other times of the year, it does not appear in gameplay or grant its effect, unless the server has Halloween mode enabled. However, it can be equipped at any time and can still be viewed on the loadout screen even when it is not visible during games.

Update history

October 27, 2011 Patch (Very Scary Halloween Special)

Bugs

When using the taunt, the Heavy's fingers sometimes form weird when he bends them.

Allows the Heavy to perform the FrankenHeavy taunt.
